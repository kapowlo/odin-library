Welcome to the online library!

Click on 'Add New Books' button and fill out the form, then click submit or reset.

The submit button will add the book while the reset button will reset all the input fields.

When you have finished reading the book, click on the 'Change Book Status' button.

you can change the book status to either "yes" when you're done reading or "no"if you aren't done reading.
